## Date Functions 
#### DATEDIFF
>DATEDIFF(INTERVAL, DATE1, DATE2)

Interval: year, month. day, week. hour. minute, ect. 

#### DATEADD 
>DATEADD(Interval, number, DATE) 
>DATE >= 'DATE' - INTERVAL 90 day 


number could be negative if wants to substract 
#### DATE_FORMAT
> DATE_FORMAT(DATE, "%Y")
> DATE_FORMAT(DATE, "%Y %M %d")
